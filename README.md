# Epson L3250 Resetter – Free Download & Service Required Fix (2026)

The **Epson L3250 printer** may display a **Service Required error** or **red light blinking problem** when the internal waste ink pad counter reaches its maximum limit.

This is a built-in protection system used by Epson printers to prevent ink overflow during long-term usage.

When this happens, the printer stops printing even though the hardware is still functioning properly.

The solution is to reset the internal counter using the **Epson L3250 Adjustment Program (Resetter Tool)**.

## Why Epson L3250 Shows Service Required Error

The error usually appears due to the following reasons:

* Waste ink pad counter overflow
* Internal maintenance counter reaching its limit
* Long-term printer usage
* Firmware protection system

Once the counter limit is reached, the printer automatically stops working until the counter is reset.

## How to Reset Epson L3250 Waste Ink Pad Counter

Follow these steps carefully:

### Step 1

Download the Epson L3250 resetter tool.

### Step 2

Extract the downloaded ZIP file.

### Step 3

Run **AdjProg.exe** as Administrator.

### Step 4

Click **Particular Adjustment Mode**.

### Step 5

Select **Waste Ink Pad Counter**.

### Step 6

Click **Check** and then press **Initialize**.

### Step 7

Turn the printer **OFF and ON again**.

Your printer should now work normally.
